NEO and GAS:
Powering the Ecosystem

NEO and GAS form the dual-token system that drives the Neo Blockchain. NEO empowers governance, giving users the ability to vote and influence key decisions about the network’s future. On the other hand, GAS fuels all on-chain operations, powering transactions, smart contracts, and decentralized applications.

With a fixed supply of 100 million indivisible NEO tokens and the divisible GAS token generated every block, this system ensures accessibility, sustainability, and active participation in the ecosystem. Together, they balance utility and governance, creating a blockchain designed for both users and developers alike.
